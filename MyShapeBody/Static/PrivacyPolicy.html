MyBodyShape Privacy Policy

Posted / Effective Date : July 23, 2017

Thanks for using MyBodyShape !
We describe below how and why we require that you takes or loads pictures using our application (both mobile and website).

Who ?

MyBodyShape is a French tech team which creates applications for the purpose of showing the mass distribution and measures of a user's body, member by member. That can help for athletics, health, clothes industry...

Where ?

MyBodyShape Team : CD 56 13790 Rousset-Sur-Arc, France
Email : mybodyshapenotifs@gmail.com
Owners and creators : Chérif Tessilimi and François Druelle

What ?

Two user's pictures are necessary. MyBodyShape is an application which simulates and computes your members weights and generates a 3d model from your real body. Your body will be analyzed with the help of two pictures, one front and one side. Only our servers have access to these pictures.

How and why ?

The application asks you to take OR load pictures. Our mathematics model needs distances between body members to work. That explains why we need two pictures.

Storage

Your two pictures are just stored for the time of simulation. When you finally generates your model, they are automatically destroyed.

Security

During the time they are stored (time of simulation which is small with good internet connection), your pictures are encrypted and secured. No data is stored for a long time or for good. No user's request access is necessary because the pictures are automatically destroyed at the end of simulation.

User data access

If you wants to check, verify or modify your data and ensure they have been deleted, please email us at bodyshapenotifs@gmail.com.

Updates

You will be notified by email for any changes about this privacy policy.
