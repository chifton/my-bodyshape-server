MyBodyShape Privacy Policy

Posted / Effective Date : July 23, 2017

Thank you for using MyBodyShape !
Please find below information (about how and why) concerning the pictures that you need to take and load when using our application (both on smartphone and website).

Who ?

MyBodyShape is a new French tech team that aims to create applications for morphometry purposes. It provides body mass distribution information to the user. Such information are particularly relevant for athletics, health, nutrition, biomechanics, clothes industry, ergonomy, etc...

Where ?

MyBodyShape Team : Aix-en-Provence, France
Email : mybodyshapenotifs@gmail.com
Owners and creators : Chérif Tessilimi and François Druelle

What ?

MyBodyShape is an application which measures, computes and simulates the mass of each body segment (i.e., the head, the arm, the hand, etc.). In the future, the application should also generate a 3D model of the user's body. At this stage, two user's pictures are needed (one front and one side) in order to perform the calculations. Be aware that only our servers have access to these pictures.

How and why ?

The application needs you to take and load two pictures because our geometric model work in the 3D space (i.e., volume). From these pictures, we use photogrammetry principles to measure body segment lengths and diameters in order to reconstruct the segment volumes and masses.

Storage

The pictures loaded in the application are just stored for the time of the simulation. When you are finally generating your results, these are automatically destroyed.

Security

When the pictures are stored, these are encrypted and secured. This stored time represents the time needed for the simulation (it is very short with good internet connection). Data are only stored for a short period. No user's request access is needed because the pictures are automatically destroyed at the end of the simulation.

User data access

If you want to check, modify or ensure that your data have been deleted, please email us at mybodyshapenotifs@gmail.com.

Updates

You will be notified by email for any changes about this privacy policy.
